# PRODUCT.md — Flex Supps

## Product Overview

**Name:** Flex Supps  
**Type:** Brand website — premium sports nutrition e-commerce  
**Market:** Algeria (DZ) — high-performance supplement store based in Cheraga, Alger  
**URL:** flexsupps.dz

## Audience

- Primary: Serious Algerian athletes, bodybuilders, and fitness enthusiasts aged 18–35
- Secondary: Fitness coaches and trainers sourcing supplements for clients
- Mindset: They value quality, authenticity, and performance above all. They are not casual buyers.

## Surface Type

**Brand** — marketing, landing, e-commerce store. Not a SaaS product UI.

## Tone & Voice

- Aggressive, cinematic, premium
- Short sentences. No fluff. No placeholder lorem ipsum.
- Commands and declarations — not questions or suggestions.
- Reference: Supreme, Nike, Palace. Not Shopify or generic DTC.

## Anti-References (what we are NOT)

- Generic Shopify supplement store with pastel cards and stock imagery
- SaaS landing page with rounded hero cards and purple-to-blue gradients
- "AI-generated" layout: icon tile above heading, card nested in card, 3-column equal grid
- Standard e-commerce with top-bar shipping announcements and predictable CTA placement

## Core Differentiators

- The ONLY premium supplement editorial experience in Algeria
- Cinematic WebGL hero, full-bleed layouts, editorial typography
- WhatsApp-native ordering flow (culturally native to DZ market)
- Curated multi-brand boutique — not a generic marketplace

## Business Goal

Convert browsers into WhatsApp orders. Every design decision must build desire, then funnel to the cart/checkout WhatsApp flow.
